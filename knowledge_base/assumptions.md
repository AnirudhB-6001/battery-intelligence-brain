# Assumptions Registry (v0)

## ASSUMP_SOH_IS_VALID_PROXY_V0
State of Health values are assumed to be comparable across the evaluated time window.

## ASSUMP_SYNTHETIC_DATA_BEHAVES_REALISTICALLY_V0
Synthetic telemetry is assumed to reflect realistic operational patterns but may not capture rare edge cases.

## ASSUMP_PRICE_CURVE_REPRESENTATIVE_V0
Market price curves used are assumed to be representative of actual settlement prices.
