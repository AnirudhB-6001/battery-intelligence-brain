# Glossary (v0)

## SoC (State of Charge)
Represents the estimated remaining charge of a battery as a percentage of usable capacity.

## SoH (State of Health)
Represents the estimated remaining usable capacity relative to nominal capacity.

## Asset
A physical or logical battery system component (site, rack, string, inverter).

## Telemetry
Time-series operational data collected from battery systems or associated equipment.
